Frankfort, KY    Monday Apr 15, 2019    USDA-KY Dept of Ag Market News

Kentucky Weekly Livestock Summary for the week of April 8-13, 2019

Receipts: 23,022			Last Week 24,673  	Last Year 27,355

   Compared to last week, feeder steers were mostly 3.00-4.00 higher and feeder 
heifers were mostly 2.00-4.00 higher with the best demand and increase noted on 
weaned and vaccinated offerings. Feeder cattle were in mostly good to very good 
demand, while unweaned and fleshy fall born calves were notably lighter in 
demand.  Slaughter cows were 2.00-4.00 higher, and slaughter bulls steady to 3.00 
higher with much improved demand and increased market activity on slaughter 
classes.

In the feeder supply, steers made up approximately 37 percent and 
heifers, approximately 44 percent.  Feeder cattle over 600 lbs totaled 
approximately 40 percent.
